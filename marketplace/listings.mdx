# Listings

Listings are the sellable layer of the marketplace.
They represent what a buyer can purchase now.

Products sit underneath listings as the underlying jersey record.
That separation lets the platform reuse product data across search, moderation, and scan workflows.
Scanner output and authenticity verdicts also feed trust decisions before a listing is published.
