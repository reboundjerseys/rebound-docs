# Marketplace Overview

Rebound Jerseys is a marketplace for jerseys.
The platform centers on a shared commerce model rather than isolated screens.

## Core marketplace concepts

- Listing: the sellable offer
- Product: the broader jersey record
- Order: the committed purchase
- Conversation: the messaging thread between participants
- Checkout draft: the pre-commit state before purchase

## What the marketplace needs from the platform

- search
- product detail pages
- seller onboarding
- checkout
- orders
- messages
- moderation
