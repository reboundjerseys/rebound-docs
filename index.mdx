# Product Overview

Rebound Jerseys is a jersey marketplace platform built around a shared commerce model.
It combines a web app, mobile app, backend API, database, search, payments, and AI-assisted image workflows.

The platform exists to support:

- discovery and shopping
- listing creation
- order management
- messaging and moderation
- warehouse intake
- scan-driven product creation
- external marketplace sync

The current implementation lives primarily in the `reboundjerseys` repository.
The scanner and authentication systems are tracked separately and are documented here as placeholders until their inventories are fully imported.

## Major engineering domains

- Marketplace and commerce
- Backend APIs and integrations
- Supabase data and storage
- Search and derived indexes
- AI image analysis and generation
- Authentication and verification
- Infrastructure and operations
